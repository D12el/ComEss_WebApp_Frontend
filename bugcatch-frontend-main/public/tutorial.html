Bug Catch

Gameplay Tutorial

Welcome to Bug Catch

Bug Catch is a web-based online game which aims to increase player click speed in the form of catching a bug.

Players have to click the bugs that appear on the screen as fast as possible in order to receive the highest possible score.

This website is a group final project of Computer Engineering Essentials class (Class of 2023), Chulalongkorn University.

The beginning of your journey

First thing first, you have to come up with your username. Please note that this will be displayed on the leaderboard if you manage to reach top 20.

Next, select your avatar of your choices.

Finally, get ready to catch'em all !

Catching the bug

The main objective of the game is to catch as many bugs as possible in a limited time.

The more score you received, more difficult the game will be. Also, You can click on a mystery box

which will give you random effects, namely: increases/decreases bug size, x1.5 score multiplier, or bug starting moving which will last temporary.

Let's compete

20 players with the highest score will have their name displayed in the hall of fame on the leaderboard.

Try your hardest to be honored, or if you already are, then be proud!

Have fun playing :D
